Monkey D. Luffy – The Future Pirate King

“I’m gonna be King of the Pirates!” – A tribute to the rubber man who sails with heart.

Here's a time line of Monkey D Luffy's Journey:

  • Childhood – Eats the Gomu Gomu no Mi and declares he’ll become Pirate King.
  • Inherits the Straw Hat – Shanks gives Luffy his straw hat as a symbol of a promise.
  • $1Leaves Foosha Village – Begins his pirate journey at age 17.
  • Meets Zoro –Forms the Straw Hat Pirates’ core.
  • Recruitment of Crew – Nami, Usopp, Sanji, Chopper, Robin, Franky, Brook, and Jinbe join him over time.
  • Arlong Park Arc – Helps free Nami’s village from the Arlong Pirates.
  • Enies Lobby – Declares war on the World Government to save Robin.
  • Impel Down & Marineford War –Tries to save Ace; suffers tragic loss.
  • Two-Year Time Skip – Trains under Rayleigh to grow stronger.
  • Wano Arc – Defeats Kaido and is recognized as one of the Four Emperors.
  • Current Goal – Reaches Egghead Island with the aim of finally learning the truth of the world and locating One Piece.

“I don’t want to conquer anything. I just think the guy with the most freedom in this whole ocean… is the Pirate King!”
--Monkey D. Luffy
